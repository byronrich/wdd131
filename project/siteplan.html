Utah National Parks Adventure

This site is for those who crave breathtaking views, unforgettable hikes, and the raw beauty of Utah’s wild landscapes. Let’s explore!

Site Purpose

To share exciting, detailed information about at least five national parks in Utah and inspire others to embark on their own adventures.

Target Audience Scenarios

  • Which Utah parks are best for first-time visitors?
  • What kinds of hikes or sights can I expect in each park?

Must-Visit National Parks

  • Zion National Park: Towering red cliffs, Angel’s Landing, and the Narrows hike.
  • Arches National Park: Iconic stone arches, sunset views, and rock climbing.
  • Bryce Canyon National Park: Hoodoos, surreal amphitheaters, and stargazing.
  • Canyonlands National Park: Deep canyons, scenic drives, and off-road adventures.
  • Capitol Reef National Park: Historic orchards, Waterpocket Fold, and hidden trails.

Color Schema

  • Desert Clay (#D2691E): Used in headings and buttons
  • Sage Green (#9CBA7F): Background elements and highlight boxes

Typography

  • Merriweather: Used for headers and titles
  • Roboto: Used for body text and descriptions

Wireframes

All content and structure follow course naming and validation conventions. This project is inspired by my own love for exploring Utah’s national parks.
