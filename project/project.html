Design Rationale

This website was designed to showcase the beauty and accessibility of Utah's National Parks. Every design choice was made with user experience, clarity, and visual appeal in mind.

Layout

The layout follows a clean, grid-based structure with a prominent hero image on the homepage to immediately capture attention. Navigation is fixed at the top for easy access, and content is organized into digestible sections with clear headings.

Color Scheme

Earth tones inspired by Utah’s natural landscapes — sandstone reds, pine greens, and sky blues — were selected to evoke a sense of place and adventure. These colors also provide strong contrast for readability.

Typography

The site uses a combination of a bold serif font for headings (to convey grandeur and tradition) and a clean sans-serif font for body text (to ensure legibility across devices).

Accessibility

All images include descriptive alt text. Color contrast meets WCAG standards, and the site is fully navigable via keyboard. Responsive design ensures usability on both desktop and mobile devices.

Responsive Design

The site adapts seamlessly to different screen sizes. On mobile, the navigation collapses into a hamburger menu, and content stacks vertically for easy scrolling.
